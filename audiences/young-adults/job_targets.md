# Job targets

A starter list of sectors and company types in the Caribbean that hire for data, ML, and AI roles. Verify each posting yourself, this list is a map not a guarantee.

## Sectors

- Banking and finance. Examples: NCB, Scotiabank Caribbean, Republic Bank, Massy Stores analytics teams.
- Telecoms. Digicel, Flow, BTC.
- BPO and tech services. itel, Sutherland, IBEX, Conduent.
- Tourism. Sandals, Marriott, regional tour operators.
- Public sector. Statistical institutes, central banks, disaster preparedness offices.
- Remote, global companies hiring Caribbean talent. Toptal, Andela, Deel based roles.

## Roles to target as a starter

- Data Analyst
- Junior Data Scientist
- Machine Learning Engineer
- AI Engineer
- Prompt Engineer or AI Solutions Engineer
- Business Intelligence Developer

## How to stand out

1. Build three Caribbean themed projects from this repo. Not generic ones.
2. Write a one page case study per project explaining the problem, your data, your model, and the business impact in dollars saved or earned.
3. Network on LinkedIn. Comment on posts from regional tech leaders. Comment, do not beg.
4. Speak at a meetup. The Caribbean tech community is small. Show up twice and people remember you.
