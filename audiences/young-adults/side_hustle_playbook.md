# Side hustle playbook

A short, honest playbook.

## Step 1, pick a niche

Pick one small group of customers. Not "small businesses", but "hairdressers in Saint Andrew" or "Airbnb hosts in Speightstown". The narrower, the easier to sell.

## Step 2, pick one offer

One problem, one solution, one price. Examples:
- WhatsApp auto reply bot for restaurants, $150 USD setup plus $30 USD per month.
- Monthly content pack of ten Instagram posts in your client's voice, $250 USD per month.
- Resume rewrite with cover letter, $80 USD.

## Step 3, build with templates first

Use the [prompt-templates](../../prompt-templates) folder. Wire a small Python script with the OpenAI or Anthropic API. Charge while you learn.

## Step 4, deliver fast and document

First three clients at half price in exchange for a testimonial. Record a Loom video showing the result.

## Step 5, raise prices

Every five clients, raise prices 20 percent. Most people undercharge for two years too long.

## Tax and legal

Speak to a local accountant. Pay your taxes. In Jamaica, register a sole trader business with COJ. In Trinidad, register at the Ministry of Legal Affairs. In Barbados, use the CAIPO. In the OECS, check the relevant commerce department. Boring work, do it.
