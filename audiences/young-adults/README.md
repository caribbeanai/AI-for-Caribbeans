# AI for Young Adults (ages 19 to 30)

You are at the stage where AI can change the trajectory of your career and your money. This track is direct, no fluff.

## Three tracks, pick one

### Track A, Get hired in AI

Goal: land a junior data or ML role at a Caribbean fintech, telco, or BPO inside twelve months.

1. Finish [courses 01 to 03](../../courses).
2. Build three portfolio projects from [applications](../../applications). Suggested: tourism demand forecast, remittance fraud detector, dengue early warning.
3. Put them on GitHub with a clean README.
4. Apply to companies listed in [job_targets.md](job_targets.md).

### Track B, Use AI in your current job

Goal: become the AI go to person at work in 90 days.

1. Read [prompt-templates](../../prompt-templates).
2. Build one automation for your team using a Claude or OpenAI API. Pick a repetitive task.
3. Document the time saved. Show it.
4. Ask for a stipend or a raise. With numbers.

### Track C, Build a side hustle

Goal: $500 USD per month in side income inside six months.

Ideas that work in our region:
- AI powered tutoring for CSEC and CAPE, charge per hour or per week.
- Local SEO and content for hotels and tour operators.
- Resume rewriting service for diaspora applicants.
- WhatsApp chatbot for small businesses, restaurants, hair salons, mechanics.
- Translate and adapt content into Caribbean voices for global brands.

See [side_hustle_playbook.md](side_hustle_playbook.md).

## Tools you should know cold

- Python, pandas, scikit-learn
- One LLM API, Claude or OpenAI
- Git and GitHub
- One cloud, AWS or Google Cloud, free tier is enough to learn
- One deployment tool, Streamlit, Vercel, or Render

## Money math

Earning USD while spending in JMD, TTD, BBD, or XCD is leverage. One small AI side gig at $500 USD per month is roughly:
- JMD 78,000 in Jamaica
- TTD 3,400 in Trinidad and Tobago
- BBD 1,000 in Barbados
- XCD 1,350 in the OECS

Open the notebook at [applications/personal-finance](../../applications/personal-finance) and plug in your real numbers.
