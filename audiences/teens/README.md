# AI for Teens (ages 13 to 18)

You already use AI every day, in TikTok recommendations, Snapchat filters, and search. Now you learn how it works and how to build with it. This track gets you from zero to a working portfolio project.

## What you will build

1. A reggae versus soca classifier from song lyrics.
2. A chatbot that answers questions in your dialect.
3. A study buddy for CSEC and CAPE.
4. A small Streamlit app you can show off.

## School project ideas

- Predict your school's lunch line wait time from the day of week and weather.
- Cluster your classmates' favourite songs and find hidden taste groups.
- Use an LLM to summarise your history notes on the Morant Bay Rebellion.
- Build a spelling helper for Bajan, Trini, Jamaican, and Kittitian dialects.

## Free resources

- Google Colab, no install required.
- Kaggle, free datasets and notebooks.
- Hugging Face, free models and a community.
- This repo, every example here is free to use and remix.

## What to avoid

- Do not paste your CSEC answers into ChatGPT and call it learning. Use it to check your work after you tried.
- Do not share personal info, even with a friendly chatbot.
- Do not believe everything an AI says. Cross check with a real source.
