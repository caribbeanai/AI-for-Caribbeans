# AI use policy, starter template

A short, sane policy any Caribbean business can adapt.

## 1. Scope

This policy covers the use of generative AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and similar) by staff of [Company Name].

## 2. Allowed

- Drafting internal documents, summaries, meeting notes.
- Brainstorming, research, learning.
- Writing and reviewing code with team approval.
- Customer facing content, with human review before sending.

## 3. Not allowed

- Pasting customer personal data, financial data, medical data, or any data classified as confidential.
- Submitting AI output as your own original analysis without disclosure.
- Using AI to make hiring, firing, lending, or disciplinary decisions without a human in the loop.
- Using AI to imitate a real person without consent.

## 5. Data handling

- Use enterprise accounts where possible. Personal accounts may train on inputs.
- Redact names, account numbers, and identifiers before pasting.

## 6. Disclosure

- AI use must be disclosed when delivering analytical or creative work to clients.
