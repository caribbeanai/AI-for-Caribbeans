# AI for Working Professionals

You have a job. You have responsibilities. You do not have six months to study. This track gets you results in 30 days.

## The 30 day plan

### Days 1 to 7, foundations

- Read [courses/01-foundations](../../courses/01-foundations). One hour per day.
- Pick one repetitive task at work. Time it. Write it down.

### Days 8 to 14, the prompt phase

- Open [prompt-templates](../../prompt-templates).
- Adapt three prompts to your job. Save them in a personal notes app.
- Use them daily.

### Days 15 to 21, the automation phase

- Take one of those prompts and wrap it in a small script. See [scripts](../../scripts).
- Connect it to your inbox, your spreadsheet, or your CRM.

### Days 22 to 30, the impact phase

- Track time saved. Be conservative.
- Write a one page memo to your boss. Title: "AI pilot results and proposal".
- Ask for budget to do more.

## By role

| Role | High value AI use |
|------|------------------|
| Accountant | Draft variance commentary, summarise audit notes, generate journal entries from receipts. |
| Lawyer | Summarise contracts, draft first cut letters, research case law, redline review. |
| Banker | Loan summary generation, KYC review, customer email drafting, fraud pattern review. |
| Teacher | Lesson plans aligned to CXC syllabus, marking rubrics, parent letters, differentiated worksheets. |
| Nurse | Patient education leaflets, shift handover summaries, drug interaction quick checks (always verify). |
| Hotel manager | Review reply drafts, demand forecasts, schedule optimisation, training material. |
| Farmer | Crop disease identification from photo, market price tracking, weather based irrigation. |
| Civil servant | Policy brief drafts, citizen letter responses, data summarisation. |

See [role_recipes.md](role_recipes.md) for a recipe per role.

Before you paste anything into an AI tool, check your employer policy. If there is none, write one. A starter template is at [ai_use_policy_template.md](ai_use_policy_template.md).
