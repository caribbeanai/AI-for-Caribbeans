# Health

Public and personal health in the Caribbean has shape that mainstream health datasets miss. Genetic predisposition to hypertension and diabetes, dengue and chikungunya outbreaks tied to rainfall, and provider shortage in rural districts.

## Projects

- [diabetes_risk.py](diabetes_risk.py) A risk screening model for Type 2 diabetes using Caribbean specific factors.
- [dengue_forecast.py](dengue_forecast.py) Early warning from rainfall and prior cases.
- [mental_health_companion_prompt.md](mental_health_companion_prompt.md) A safe, warm chatbot prompt.

## Important note

AI is a screening and education tool. It is not a diagnosis. Every output must point a user toward a qualified health worker. Build with clinicians, not around them.
