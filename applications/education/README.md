# Education

The CSEC and CAPE exams shape young Caribbean lives. AI gives a one to one tutor to every student who has a phone.

## Projects

- [csec_question_generator.py](csec_question_generator.py) Generate practice questions from a syllabus topic.
- [marking_assistant_prompt.md](marking_assistant_prompt.md) A teacher's marking helper.
- [reading_buddy_prompt.md](reading_buddy_prompt.md) A patient reading buddy for early readers.

## How to integrate AI into a classroom

1. Start with one teacher, one class, one subject, one term.
2. Use AI for: lesson plans, rubrics, practice questions, parent letters.
3. Do not use AI for: marking that affects grades without teacher review, public judgements about students.
4. Teach students to use AI honestly. The cheating debate is a teaching opportunity.
