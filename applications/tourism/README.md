# Tourism

Tourism is the largest export earner for many Caribbean nations. AI sharpens demand forecasts, automates guest communication, prices rooms intelligently, and reads sentiment fast.

## Projects

- [arrivals_forecast.py](arrivals_forecast.py) Monthly stayover forecasts.
- [review_sentiment.py](review_sentiment.py) Sentiment plus aspect tagging.
- [dynamic_pricing.py](dynamic_pricing.py) A first cut room pricing model.
- [itinerary_generator_prompt.md](itinerary_generator_prompt.md) LLM prompt for personalised itineraries.

## Where AI pays off fastest

1. Replying to reviews on TripAdvisor, Google, and Booking. Faster, better, more consistent.
2. Forecasting demand 60 to 120 days out for staffing and inventory.
3. Dynamic pricing of last 10 percent of inventory.
4. Multilingual front desk chat for Spanish, French, Dutch, and Portuguese arrivals.
5. Operations analytics, room turnover times, F and B sales by hour.

## Caution

A model that overprices a room in low season can leave it empty. A model that underprices in high season leaves money on the table. Always set human bounds.
