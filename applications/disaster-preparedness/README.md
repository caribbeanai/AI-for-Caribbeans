# Disaster Preparedness

Hurricanes, earthquakes, floods, volcanic activity. The Caribbean lives with these. AI helps with prediction, routing, and damage assessment, but the foundations are still people, plans, and supplies.

## Projects

- [evacuation_router.py](evacuation_router.py) A shortest path router with shelter capacity constraints.
- [damage_assessment_prompt.md](damage_assessment_prompt.md) Prompt for drone or phone photo damage scoring.
- [shelter_demand_forecast.py](shelter_demand_forecast.py) Forecast shelter occupancy.

## Build with ODPEM, NEMO, NEMA, CDEMA

National disaster offices exist in every Caribbean country. Anything you build should plug into their plans, not replace them. The Caribbean Disaster Emergency Management Agency (CDEMA) coordinates regional response.

## Household checklist

- Three days of food and water per person, minimum.
- Important documents in a sealed plastic bag.
- Charged power bank and a small radio.
- Cash. ATMs fail when grid does.
- A meeting point if phones are down.
