# Agriculture

Caribbean agriculture is full of opportunity for AI. We are small enough that one good model can move a whole industry.

## Projects

- [yield_predictor.py](yield_predictor.py) Predict yam yield from rainfall, parish, and inputs.
- [pest_id_prompt.md](pest_id_prompt.md) A prompt for plant disease identification from a photo.
- [market_price_alert.py](market_price_alert.py) Track local market prices and alert on unusual spikes.

## Use cases by crop

| Crop | AI opportunity |
|------|---------------|
| Yam | Yield forecasting, market timing |
| Banana | Disease detection (Black Sigatoka), packhouse sorting |
| Sugar | Burn scheduling, mill throughput optimisation |
| Cocoa | Flavour profile from image and fermentation logs |
| Coffee (Blue Mountain) | Quality grading from image, fraud detection |
| Coconut | Yield mapping from satellite imagery |
| Pepper | Pest pressure forecasting |
| Fisheries | Sustainable catch limits, illegal fishing detection |

## Notes

- Smallholder data is messy. Build for paper records first, app records second.
- Voice notes in Kreyol, Patois, or Spanish are often more useful than typed input. Lean into that.
- Partner with extension officers. They are the bridge between you and the farmer.
