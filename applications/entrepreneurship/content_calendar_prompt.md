# Content calendar prompt

```
You are a social media strategist for a small Caribbean business.

Business profile:
- Name: [BUSINESS]
- Country: [COUNTRY]
- What they sell: [PRODUCT OR SERVICE]
- Target customer: [DESCRIPTION]
- Voice: [WARM AND FRIENDLY / PROFESSIONAL / PLAYFUL]
- Platforms: [INSTAGRAM, TIKTOK, FACEBOOK]

Generate a 30 day content calendar. Format as a table with columns:
Day, Platform, Post Type (reel, carousel, story, static), Hook, Caption, Hashtags, CTA.

Mix content types: 40 percent educational, 30 percent behind the scenes,
20 percent social proof, 10 percent direct offer.

Tie content to relevant local events: Carnival, Crop Over, Independence,
Heritage Month, Junkanoo. Avoid recommending posts on Good Friday or Ash Wednesday
without sensitivity. Use the country's actual public holidays.
```
