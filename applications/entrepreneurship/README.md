# Entrepreneurship

For the small business owner, the solo founder, the side hustler. AI is leverage. One person can now do the work of a team.

## Projects

- [pricing_calculator.py](pricing_calculator.py) Compute a defensible service price.
- [content_calendar_prompt.md](content_calendar_prompt.md) Generate a month of social media content.
- [whatsapp_bot_starter.py](whatsapp_bot_starter.py) Scaffold for a WhatsApp customer support bot.

## Where to apply AI in your business this week

1. Customer support replies. Save two hours a day.
2. Social media content. One month of posts in one hour.
3. Inventory and price tracking. Automate the spreadsheet you already maintain.
4. Lead qualification. Build a small form plus AI scoring.
5. Quotes and invoices. Generate first drafts from a brief.

## Common founder mistakes

- Trying to automate everything before doing anything by hand.
- Building before selling. Sell the result, then build the system.
- Hiding behind the bot. Customers want a human on hard problems.
- Skipping the basics. AI does not fix a bad offer.
