# Personal Finance

Money decisions for Caribbean realities. Multi currency thinking, remittance flows, savings on island incomes, and how to use AI to manage it all.

## Projects

- [budget_assistant.py](budget_assistant.py) A spending classifier and monthly summary in your local currency.
- [remittance_optimiser.py](remittance_optimiser.py) Pick the cheapest channel for a USD to JMD transfer.
- [savings_goal_simulator.py](savings_goal_simulator.py) How long to a goal, including small monthly amounts.

## Cost of living quick view

For someone earning USD 1,500 per month remotely, an indicative monthly budget:

| Country | Currency | Rent (1BR, decent area) | Food and basics | Transport | Net savings target |
|--------|---------|--------------------------|----------------|-----------|--------------------|
| Jamaica | JMD | ~95,000 | ~70,000 | ~25,000 | ~25,000 |
| Trinidad and Tobago | TTD | ~4,500 | ~3,500 | ~1,500 | ~1,000 |
| Barbados | BBD | ~2,200 | ~1,800 | ~600 | ~400 |
| Saint Lucia (OECS) | XCD | ~3,000 | ~2,400 | ~800 | ~500 |
| Bahamas | BSD | ~1,400 | ~1,100 | ~400 | ~300 |

Indicative only. Real numbers depend on lifestyle and parish or district.

## AI for everyday money

- Use an LLM to read your bank statement PDF and categorise spend.
- Use an LLM to draft a polite reply to a debt collector.
- Use a small script to alert you when JMD weakens past a threshold.
- Build a chatbot for your susu group that tracks who has paid.
