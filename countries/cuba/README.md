# Cuba

Country profile for AI for Caribbeans.

## Quick facts

- **Capital:** Havana
- **Currency:** Cuban Peso (CUP)
- **Languages:** Spanish (official)
- **Population:** about 11 million
- **Area:** 109,884 km^2
- **Independence:** 20 May 1902
- **National dish:** Ropa vieja, congrí, lechón
- **Music:** Son, mambo, rumba, salsa, timba

## Dialect and language

A small sample of common phrases:

- Asere -> friend
- ¿Qué bolá? -> What's up?
- Pinchar -> to work
- Coger botella -> to hitchhike
- Acere, qué volá -> hey buddy what's up

## Five facts you should know

- The largest island in the Caribbean.
- Old Havana is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- Cuba has one of the highest doctor to patient ratios in the world.
- Birthplace of salsa, son, and the mojito.

## AI use case tailored to Cuba

Offline first educational AI tutors that work without reliable internet, important in much of the country. Edge models on cheap Android phones can serve schools.

## Suggested datasets

Public Cuban statistical yearbooks, regional health data.
