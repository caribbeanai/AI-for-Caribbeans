# Capstone case study template

One page. Be specific. Be honest.

## Title

[A one line statement of what you predict]

## Problem

Who has this problem. What goes wrong today. What it costs.

## Data

Source, size, columns, time range. Any cleaning. Anything you wished you had.

## Approach

Model family. Why this one and not another. Features you used.

## Results

Key metric, on the held out test set. Compare to a simple baseline.

## Business impact

In dollars per month or per year. Be conservative. Show the math.

## Limits and next steps

What this model cannot do. What to try next.

## Reproducing

A short list of commands so someone else can run your work.
