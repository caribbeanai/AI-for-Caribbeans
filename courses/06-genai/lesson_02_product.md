# Lesson 2, Building a small AI product

A weekend product. The shortest path from idea to a paying user.

## Pick a wedge

A wedge is a specific person with a specific problem they will pay to solve.

Not "I help businesses with AI."
But "I help small hotels in Negril reply to guest reviews in 90 seconds."

## Build in three layers

1. **Prompt.** Get the output quality right in one prompt first.
2. **App.** Wrap it in a 50 line Streamlit page.
3. **Payment.** Add Stripe with a 7 day trial. Charge from day one.

## The 48 hour rule

If you cannot ship a v1 in 48 hours of focused work, the scope is too big. Cut.

## Distribution

- Post in a Facebook group your target customers actually read.
- Send 30 personalised messages on LinkedIn.
- Offer a free audit, deliver via your product.
- One in ten becomes a paying customer at first. That is fine.

## Pricing

Charge monthly. Three tiers, only show the middle one in big print. Start at $19, $49, $129 USD per month. Adjust up after ten paying customers.
