## Capstone

Ship one small AI product that serves a Caribbean audience. Possible products:

- A WhatsApp study buddy for CSEC students.
- A tour itinerary generator for first time Caribbean travellers.
- A document summariser for small law firms.
- A recipe transformer that adapts global recipes to local ingredients.
- A "Granny's stories" app that records and transcribes elder stories in dialect.

Real users, real feedback, charge for it if possible.
