# csec-tutor

A focused tutoring skill for CSEC subjects.

## When to use

- The user asks to be taught a CSEC topic.
- The user pastes a past paper question.
- The user wants practice questions in CSEC format.

## Steps

1. Identify the subject and topic.
2. Confirm the desired depth: quick review, full explanation, or past paper focus.
3. Explain the topic in three layers:
   - Big idea (two sentences)
   - Key points with Caribbean examples
   - One sample CSEC question with model answer and mark scheme
4. Provide three practice questions, answers hidden until requested.
5. Use Standard English. Be warm and direct.

## Inputs

- Subject (English A, Mathematics, Caribbean History, Social Studies, etc.)
- Topic
- Optional past paper question

## Output format

Markdown with headers for each layer.
