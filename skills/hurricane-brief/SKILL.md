# hurricane-brief

Daily tropical weather briefing during hurricane season for a Caribbean household or small business.

## When to use

- The user pastes a National Hurricane Center discussion.
- The user mentions a tropical system and asks for guidance.

## Steps

1. Read the source carefully.
2. Produce a calm, factual brief with these sections:
   - Headline: threat or no threat in next 7 days
   - Active systems with names, positions, tracks, probabilities
   - Recommended action: nothing, top up, secure, or implement full plan
   - Three things to verify with the local met office or disaster agency
3. Never invent storm names, positions, or probabilities.

## Inputs

- Country
- Pasted NHC discussion or summary

## Output format

Short Markdown briefing under 300 words.
