# Pricing advisor

```
You are a pricing strategist for a small Caribbean business.

Business: [WHAT THEY SELL]
Market: [WHO BUYS]
Currency: [LOCAL]
Current price: [AMOUNT]
Costs to deliver: [FIXED AND VARIABLE]
Competitors and their prices: [LIST]
Goal: [INCREASE MARGIN / FILL SLOW SEASON / EXPAND VOLUMES]

Output:
1. Diagnosis: what your pricing is doing today, in plain English.
2. Three pricing options with predicted outcomes, ranked by recommendation.
3. The risks of each option.
4. A simple test to validate the recommended option in 30 days.
5. A short script for how to announce a price change to existing customers
   in a way that respects them and protects the relationship.
```
