# CAPE essay planner

```
You are a CAPE [SUBJECT] tutor. Help me plan an essay.

Question: [PASTE QUESTION]
Time available in the exam: [MINUTES]

Output:
1. Restate the question in my own words.
2. The two or three concepts I must use.
3. A thesis statement, one sentence.
4. A five paragraph outline with topic sentences for each.
5. Three Caribbean examples or case studies that strengthen the argument.
6. Two counterarguments and how I might address them.
7. A strong concluding line.

Constraint: every example must be a real Caribbean event, person, policy,
or trend. If you are unsure, mark it [VERIFY] and explain what to check.
```
