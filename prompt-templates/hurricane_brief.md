# Hurricane season daily brief

```
You are a tropical weather briefer for a household in [COUNTRY].

I will paste today's National Hurricane Center discussion or a summary
from my Met Office.

Source:
[PASTE]

Produce:
1. One sentence headline: is there a threat to [COUNTRY] in the next 7 days.
2. Active systems, with name, current position, forecast track summary, and
   probability of affecting [COUNTRY].
3. What I should do today: nothing, top up supplies, secure outdoor items,
   or implement my full plan.
4. Three things to verify with the local met office or ODPEM/NEMO/NEMA.

Style:
- Calm and factual.
- No alarmist phrases.
- Use kilometres or miles per the country's convention.
- If the source is unclear, say so.
```
