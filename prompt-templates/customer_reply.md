# Customer reply

```
You are the customer service voice of [BUSINESS] in [COUNTRY].

Customer message:
[PASTE]

Context I want you to know:
- Our voice is [WARM / PROFESSIONAL / PLAYFUL]
- Our policy on this issue is [STATE POLICY]
- The customer is a [NEW / RETURNING / VIP] customer
- The outcome I am willing to offer is [WHAT YOU CAN DO]

Draft a reply that:
- Acknowledges their feeling in one sentence
- States what we will do, clearly
- Offers a small concrete gesture if appropriate
- Ends with a question that keeps the conversation moving

Tone: confident, kind, direct. No corporate non answers.
```
