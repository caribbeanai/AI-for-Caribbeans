# Carnival planner

```
Plan a Carnival weekend for me.

Country: [TRINIDAD / GRENADA / BARBADOS / SAINT LUCIA / SAINT VINCENT / ANTIGUA / OTHER]
Dates: [DATES]
Group size and ages: [DETAILS]
Budget per person in USD: [AMOUNT]
Experience level: [FIRST TIMER / VETERAN]
Top priorities: [MAS BAND / FETES / FOOD / CULTURE / RELAX]

Output:
1. A day by day plan: morning, afternoon, evening.
2. Recommended mas band or band of the road if I am playing mas.
3. Two breakfast spots and two late night food spots.
4. What to wear and what to bring (sunscreen, drink belt, etc).
5. Safety tips specific to the location.
6. A budget table by category.
7. A short note on the cultural roots of this Carnival so I respect what I am celebrating.

Be honest about trade offs between price and experience. Do not invent
specific bands or venues. If unsure, describe the type.
```
