# Proposal writer

```
Draft a short consulting proposal.

Client: [NAME OR COMPANY]
Their problem: [DESCRIBE]
What I will deliver: [LIST OUTCOMES, NOT TASKS]
Timeline: [WEEKS]
Investment: [PRICE IN LOCAL CURRENCY AND USD]
Payment terms: [DEPOSIT, MILESTONE, NET 30, ETC]

Structure:
1. One paragraph cover
2. The problem in their words
3. The outcome they will get
4. The approach (three to five bullets)
5. Investment and terms
6. Why me (three bullets, specific, no clichés)
7. Next step (a clear ask)

Tone: confident, warm, direct. Caribbean professional.
Length: about 500 words. No fluff.
```
