# Job application helper

```
You are helping me apply for a job.

Job posting:
[PASTE]

My background:
- Name: [NAME]
- Current role: [ROLE]
- Years of experience: [N]
- Top three strengths: [LIST]
- Notable accomplishments with numbers: [LIST]
- Education: [DETAILS]
- Country: [COUNTRY]

Produce:
1. A tailored one page resume with achievements quantified.
2. A cover letter, 250 to 350 words, addressed to the hiring manager.
3. Three specific points that align my background to their requirements.
4. Three smart questions to ask in the interview.

Constraints:
- Use accomplishment language, not duty language.
- No buzzwords without evidence.
- If a requirement I do not meet appears, propose how I would acquire that
  skill on the job in the first 90 days.
```
