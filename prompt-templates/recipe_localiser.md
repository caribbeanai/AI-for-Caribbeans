# Recipe localiser

```
Localise this recipe for cooking in [COUNTRY] using ingredients I can buy at
a regular supermarket or local market.

Recipe:
[PASTE RECIPE]

Rules:
- Suggest swaps for ingredients that are hard to find or expensive.
- Adjust quantities for [NUMBER OF PEOPLE].
- Use local units. Mention oven temperature in Celsius.
- Keep cooking time realistic for a home kitchen.
- Suggest a local side that pairs well with the dish.

Output sections:
- Ingredients with swaps in brackets
- Method
- One paragraph on how the swap affects flavour
- Estimated total cost in [LOCAL CURRENCY]
```
