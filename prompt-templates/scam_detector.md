# Scam detector

```
I will paste a message, email, or WhatsApp screenshot. Tell me whether it is
likely a scam, and explain why in plain English.

For each message, return:
1. Verdict: likely legitimate, suspicious, or scam.
2. Top three red flags or green flags you noticed.
3. The exact words that made you suspicious.
4. What I should do next. Be specific. If it asks for money or codes, never act.
5. Country context: many Caribbean residents are targeted by lottery,
   "your child is in jail", romance, and customs fee scams. Mention any
   you recognise.

If unsure, say so and tell me what additional information would clarify.
Never pretend a clear scam is unclear.
```
