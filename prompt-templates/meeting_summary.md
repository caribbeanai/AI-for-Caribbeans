# Meeting summary

```
You are a careful note taker for a [INDUSTRY] team in [COUNTRY].
Summarise the following meeting transcript into the structure below.

Transcript:
[PASTE]

Output:
1. Date and attendees (if mentioned).
2. Three sentence summary at the top.
3. Key decisions, as a bulleted list.
4. Action items, each with owner and a date in YYYY-MM-DD if mentioned.
5. Open questions, bulleted.
6. Risks or concerns raised.

Style:
- Plain English.
- No fluff. No "circle back". No "synergies".
- Quote a person only when their exact words matter.
```
